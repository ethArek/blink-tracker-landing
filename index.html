FAQ

Answers to the common questions people have before installing.

Does it record or upload my video?

No. The app analyzes frames locally to estimate blink events. It doesn’t need an account and doesn’t upload video by default.

What data does it save?

Blink events and aggregates are stored locally (SQLite) in your chosen output directory, along with rotating log files. You can optionally export aggregates to CSV/JSON for your own analysis.

How do reminders work?

If no blink is detected for a set amount of time, the app plays a reminder sound and repeats it at an interval until you blink again. You can tune timings or disable alerts entirely.

My blinks are missed (or it’s too sensitive). What should I do?

Try adjusting the eye-aspect-ratio threshold and the consecutive-frame requirement. Higher thresholds are more sensitive; more consecutive frames can reduce false positives.

What if my camera won’t open?

Ensure your OS granted camera permissions, close other apps using the camera, and try a different camera index if you have multiple devices.

What are the requirements?

A working webcam and camera permissions. If you run from source, you’ll also need Python 3.10+ and a desktop session for the live preview window.
